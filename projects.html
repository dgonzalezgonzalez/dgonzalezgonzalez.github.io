OECD consultancy

Demand-side policies for sustainable food choices

Part-time consulting work for the OECD Trade and Agriculture Directorate on sustainable food choices, policy design, environmental impacts, and consumer behavior. I developed a Python RAG tool using local LLM and embedding models to structure policy information and construct semantic policy-alignment indices for further analysis.
