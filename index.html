Research

Papers and policy work

Published Papers

The impact of obesity on human capital accumulation: Exploring the driving factors

With Raquel Carrasco. Economics & Human Biology, 2025.

Studies how childhood obesity affects academic performance and cognitive skills, with evidence on mental health, time preferences, and expected labor-market discrimination as mechanisms.

Working Papers

Five-Year Impacts of High School Financial Education on Knowledge, Preferences and Schooling Decisions

With Margarita Machelett and Ernesto Villanueva. Working paper, 2025.

Evaluates long-run effects of a randomized high-school financial education program in Spain on financial literacy, patience, schooling choices, and expected returns to education.

Bad Apples, Social Capital and Human Capital in El Salvador: Evidence from Salvadoran High School Students

Master thesis and working paper, 2024.

Examines classroom conflict, social capital, gang activity, and bullying as determinants of cognitive performance, schooling expectations, and persistence among students in El Salvador.

Policy Reports

The potential effects of reducing food loss and waste: Impacts on the triple challenge and cost-benefits analysis

With Claude Nenert, Celine Giner, Stephan Hubertus Gay and Armelle Elasri. OECD working paper, 2025.

Uses Aglink-Cosimo scenario analysis and cost estimates to study food loss and waste reduction, agricultural income, emissions, food security, and politically feasible targets.

Beyond Food Loss and Waste Reduction Targets: Translating Reduction Ambitions Into Policy Outcomes

With Celine Giner, Morvarid Bagherzadeh, Armelle Elasri, Kotone Yamamoto and Cecil Haverkamp. OECD policy paper, 2025.

Reviews the food loss and waste policy environment using evidence collected from 42 national ministries and the European Commission.
